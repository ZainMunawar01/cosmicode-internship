ICT Club Counselors

ICT Club Counselors are dedicated student representatives who play a vital role in the success of the ICT Club at MNS-UET. They are responsible for leading key operations, mentoring members, organizing events, and building a strong tech-driven community. Their efforts ensure that the club remains dynamic, impactful, and aligned with the latest advancements in technology and innovation.

Become an ICT Club Counselor

ICT Club Counselors will be selected based on their dedication, skills, and active involvement in the ICT Club. Only those who demonstrate a strong commitment to leadership, innovation, and student engagement will be eligible for this prestigious role.

Each counselor is expected to commit to the role for a full academic year and play an active part in club initiatives. This includes participation in events, mentorship activities, and continuous contributions towards fostering a thriving tech community.

Are you driven by technology, leadership, and innovation? Do you want to play a key role in shaping the future of ICT at MNS-UET? As an ICT Club Counselor, you will be part of an elite leadership team responsible for managing high-impact events, mentoring students, and fostering strong industry collaborations.

This is your opportunity to gain recognition, professional growth, and exclusive networking with industry experts. Join the ICT Club Leadership Team and make an impact that lasts beyond your university years.
